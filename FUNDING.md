# Fund

Mochi is, and always will be, free and open source under the MIT License.
There is no "open core", no premium tier, no enterprise edition.

## Sponsorship

the project. Sponsorship funds development, infrastructure, and security audits.

## Where the money goes

- **Maintainer time** — paying the lead maintainer and active contributors for their work.
- **Infrastructure** — CI runners, package registries, and the documentation site.
- **Security** — funding annual security audits by an independent firm.
- **Events** — sending contributors to conferences (GopherCon, CharmConf, etc.) to represent the project.

## Transparency

We publish a quarterly financial report on this page. Total income, total
expenses, and a breakdown of how the money was spent.

## Governance

Sponsorship does not buy influence. All technical decisions are made by the
core maintainer team based on what is best for the project and its users.
Sponsors are listed in the [SPONSORS.md](SPONSORS.md) file with their permission.

## Thanks
